Strategic Overview

Bridging the STEM Gap

Malaysia has thousands of STEM graduates, but many are underemployed. At the same time, students in schools lack the tools and equipment for real, hands-on science learning.

Cipta Future Lab (CFL) bridges this gap. We hire graduates as Science Executives to lead interactive workshops in schools, bringing science to life through mobile lab kits.

Core Objective

"Solve graduate unemployment while inspiring the next generation of Malaysian innovators."

National Challenge

A Two-Fold Crisis

The Talent Waste

According to Khazanah Research Institute, skills mismatch is rising. STEM graduates are increasingly forced into unrelated service industries, leading to a loss of technical confidence and the underutilization of national human capital.

  • No technical teaching pathways
  • Limited R&D laboratory entry points
Source: KRI Skills Report

Educational Fatigue

While science is compulsory, Ministry of Education reports highlight a lack of functioning labs and teacher time constraints. This results in science being perceived as boring, difficult, and irrelevant to real life.

  • Textbook-reliant memorization
  • Insufficient equipment for 1:1 engagement
Source: MOE STEM Empowerment Plan
